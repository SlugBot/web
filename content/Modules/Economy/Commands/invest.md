# `/invest`
Invest SC in text channels
*Module: [[Economy]]*
## `/invest add`
Make a new investment
#### channel
Channel to invest in
- Type: `CHANNEL`
- Required: `true`
#### amount
Amount of SC to invest
- Type: `NUMBER`
- Required: `false`
## `/invest terminate`
Terminate an existing investment prematurely
#### channel
Channel to terminate investment in
- Type: `Autocomplete Selection`
- Required: `true`
