# `/persistentrole`
*Module: [[Moderation]]*<br>
Add or remove persistent roles
## `/persistentrole add`
Add persistent role
#### role
role to make persistent
- Type: `ROLE`
- Required: `true`
## `/persistentrole remove`
Remove persistent role
#### role
Remove role as persistent
- Type: `ROLE`
- Required: `true`
## `/persistentrole list`
List all persistent roles in this guild
