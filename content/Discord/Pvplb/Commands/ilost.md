# `/ilost`
*Module: [[Pvplb]]*<br>
declare the outcome of a challenge as a loss
## `/ilost <rounds_won> <rounds_lost>`
#### rounds_won
Number of rounds you won
- Type: `INTEGER`
- Required: `true`
## `/ilost <rounds_won> <rounds_lost>`
#### rounds_lost
Number of rounds you lost
- Type: `INTEGER`
- Required: `true`
