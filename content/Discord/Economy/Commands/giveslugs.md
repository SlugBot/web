# `/giveslugs`
*Module: [[Economy]]*<br>
Transfer slugs from your balance to another user
## `/giveslugs <target> <amount>`
#### target
Recipient member to transfer slugs to
- Type: `USER`
- Required: `true`
## `/giveslugs <target> <amount>`
#### amount
Amount to transfer
- Type: `NUMBER`
- Required: `true`
