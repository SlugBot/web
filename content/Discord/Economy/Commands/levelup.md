# `/levelup`
*Module: [[Economy]]*<br>
Level up a stat
## `/levelup <stat> [number_of_levels]`
#### stat
Stat to level up
  - firewall
  - bypasser
  - encryption
  - cracker
  - scanner
  - interceptor
  - wire
  - injector
- Required: `true`
## `/levelup <stat> [number_of_levels]`
#### number_of_levels
If remaining stat levels from a respecc, number of levels to increase stat by
- Type: `INTEGER`
- Required: `false`
