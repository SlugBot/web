# `/rank`
*Module: [[ActivityExp]]*<br>
View your own or another's profile and rank
## `/rank [member] [rank] [lb_type]`
#### member
View a member's profile and rank
- Type: `USER`
- Required: `false`
## `/rank [member] [rank] [lb_type]`
#### rank
View member at a specific rank
- Type: `INTEGER`
- Required: `false`
## `/rank [member] [rank] [lb_type]`
#### lb_type
weekly or overall rank
  - All time
  - Weekly
- Required: `false`
